Simulation of Automatic light

The home automation IoT project successfully integrated various sensors and devices to enhance household efficiency and safety. The automation of a bulb and a DC motor demonstrates practical applications in lighting and appliance control. Utilizing a PIR sensor for motion detection, an ultrasonic distance sensor for object proximity, a gas sensor for air quality monitoring, and photoresistors for light level adjustments, the system showcases comprehensive environmental responsiveness. The PIR sensor improves security by detecting movement, while the ultrasonic sensor aids in precise distance measurement. The gas sensor ensures safety by monitoring potentially hazardous gases, and the photoresistors optimize lighting based on ambient light conditions. This project illustrates the potential of IoT in creating smarter, safer, and more efficient homes, paving the way for future enhancements and broader applications in home automation technology.
